Category Searching

Sometimes you just need to be able to limit your results by their category too. With Sift that's as easy as pie.

Limiting by category can be broken into two parts : 1. Listing your categories and 2. Passing your Categories.

1. Listing Your Categories

To list, just use the standard EE category tag syntax within the sift:form tag:

..
	<select name=" category">
		<option></option>
		{exp:channel:categories cat_group="1"}
		<option value="{cat_id}">{category_name}</option>
		{/exp:channel:categories}
	</select>
..

2. Passing your Categories

You can pass your categories on two two differently named parameters. The simplest is just 'category'. If you need to pass multiple categories and have them available for form repopulation, you can pass them in multiple parameters, with names of the form 'category_group_#.

The actual under-the-hood implemenation of those parameters are the same, but you may need to split the input out for your users.

For Reference:

There are two parameters you can pass to help limit your searches by categories:

category
Limit your searches to specific categories. Pass the category id, or list of category ids pipe delimited.
category_group_#
Allows you to pass independant category ids for different category groups. Works in exactly the same way as the category param.
Simply pass like - category_group_1="12" category_group_4="42" etc..
This lets you easily repopulate your search form later on.
